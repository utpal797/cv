Recipie for maggie!
  1. Heat the pan.
  2. Take 2 spoon oil.
  3. Add all diferent spices along with the one you get in the maggie packet to it.
  4. Add following veggies if available:
    • Potato😍
    • Tomato😘
    • Cabbage💕
    • Capsicum😊
  5. Add 3 cup water to it.
  6. Add maggie to it
  7. Boil for 2-5 minutes
  8. Your maggie is ready.
